The work服务说明

云博文书 is a one-person academic writing consultancy. I work with graduate applicants writing personal statements and SOPs, researchers who need papers or dissertations edited for publication, PhD students preparing supervisor inquiry letters, and professionals who need academic English support of any kind. 云博文书是一家独立学术写作咨询工作室，由一人运营。服务对象包括：撰写个人陈述和目的陈述的研究生申请者、需要润色论文或毕业论文以供发表的研究人员、准备导师联系信的博士生，以及任何需要学术英文支持的专业人士。

Every document is written by me personally. No AI tools are used at any stage — not for drafting, not for editing. No templates. No junior writers. You'll hear back from me directly — I aim to respond within 24 hours. 每份文书均由我本人亲自撰写，全程不使用AI工具，没有模板，没有初级写手。你将直接收到我的回复——我的目标是在24小时内回复。
