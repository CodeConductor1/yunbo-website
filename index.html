Research Proposal — Carbon AccountingResearch Proposal

In recent decades, the disclosure of accounting information has gained growing significance within the realm of accounting research, which, partly due to transparency efforts, but also because of the heightened importance of environmental protection, has led to a situation where individuals express apprehension regarding whether corporations are actively addressing the emerging environmental crisis. In recent years, China put forward a regulatory policy of building an "ecological civilization" (Yu and Tsai, 2018).

Furthermore, there is a clear imperative to introduce innovative accounting practices, as well as developing more familiarity with corporate environmental transparency, analysis, and auditing.

Research Proposal — Carbon AccountingResearch Proposal

Corporate environmental accountability has transitioned from a public relations choice to a core structural variable in corporate financial reporting. With China's explicit mandate to build an "ecological civilization," enterprise-level low-carbon transitions have become a baseline institutional constraint rather than a voluntary preference (Yu and Tsai, 2018).

Developing advanced expertise in green accounting methodologies and sustainability auditing has emerged as an essential next step in this research agenda.

How it works合作流程

Everything is handled directly between us — no intermediaries, no outsourcing.全程由我本人跟进，不经任何中间环节，不外包给任何人。

1
Get in touch发起咨询
Reach out via the contact form, email, WeChat, or Xiaohongshu. Briefly describe what you need — I reply within 24–36 hours.通过表单、邮件、微信或小红书联系我，简单描述你的情况，我会在24至36小时内回复。
2
Background intake素材采集
I send you a detailed questionnaire covering your background, goals, and target programmes. This is the foundation everything is built on.我会发送一份详细问卷，了解你的背景、目标和申请项目——这是后续所有写作的基础。
3
First draft撰写初稿
I write everything personally, without AI tools. You receive a complete draft — not an outline, not a template.所有内容均由我本人亲自撰写，不使用AI工具。你收到的是完整初稿，不是大纲，不是模板。
4
Revisions修改打磨
You give feedback, I revise. Rounds included vary by service and tier — see each service for details.你提出反馈，我进行修改。各项服务包含的修改次数不同，详见各项说明。
5
Final delivery交付定稿
The finished document is yours. Submit it, and let me know how it goes.定稿交付给你。提交申请，有消息记得告诉我。

Common questions常见问题

Do you write from scratch, or do you only edit existing drafts?你是全程写作，还是只润色我已有的草稿？
Both. Every service comes at three tiers:

Edit — language corrections on your draft
Rewrite — full structural and language overhaul
Full Writing — from scratch, built on a detailed intake两种都做。每项服务分三个层级：

润色（Edit）——对你的草稿进行语言修正
重写（Rewrite）——结构与语言全面改写
全程写作（Full Writing）——从零开始，基于详细素材采集
How do I know you won't use AI?你说不用AI，我怎么确认？
Every document is built entirely from what you share with me — your background, experiences, academic trajectory, and goals. All materials are professionally written to meet the academic standards of the institutions you are applying to.每份文书都完全建立在你提供的信息上——你的背景、经历、学术轨迹和目标。这种层次的个人细节只能收集，无法生成。所有材料均按照目标院校的学术诚信要求撰写。
What's your turnaround time?交稿需要多长时间？
Standard turnaround for a first draft is 5–7 working days after intake materials are received. Rush orders (3 working days) are available at +50%, subject to availability. For packages with multiple documents, we agree a schedule at the start.收到素材后，标准交稿时间为5至7个工作日。加急服务（3个工作日）另加50%，视档期而定。套餐含多份文书的，我们会在开始时确认时间安排。
Is my information kept confidential?我的信息和文书内容会保密吗？
Yes. Everything you share with me is used only to write your documents. Nothing is shared with third parties, and I do not keep or reuse your materials after the engagement ends.是的。你提供给我的所有信息只用于撰写你的文书，不会与任何第三方共享。合作结束后，我不会留存或重复使用你的材料。
Can you work with my deadline?你能配合我的截止日期吗？
Usually yes — get in touch as early as possible. Tell me your deadline upfront and I'll tell you honestly whether it works.大多数情况下可以——尽量早联系我。把你的截止日期告诉我，我会直接说行不行。
I'm not sure which service or package I need. Can you advise?我不确定该选哪项服务或套餐，可以咨询吗？
Yes — just reach out and describe your situation. I'll tell you what I think you actually need, not what's most expensive.可以——直接联系我，描述你的情况就行。我会告诉你我觉得你真正需要什么，而不是推荐最贵的那个。
